A Word From Our Advisor

Advisor's Note

"

It is with great pride that I introduce EduCycle, a student-led social business initiative that demonstrates how innovative ideas can create meaningful and lasting social impact. EduCycle addresses two important challenges facing our communities today: improving mathematics education for young learners and reducing plastic waste through creative recycling.

By transforming discarded plastic bottle caps into interactive Eco-Learning Kits, EduCycle provides children with an engaging and hands-on approach to learning mathematics while promoting environmental responsibility. This initiative reflects the belief that education and sustainability can work hand in hand to build a better future.

What makes EduCycle truly remarkable is the passion, creativity, and commitment demonstrated by our students. They have embraced the principles of social business by developing a solution that not only addresses real societal needs but also strives to be sustainable and scalable. Their work exemplifies the values of innovation, collaboration, empathy, and responsible leadership that we seek to cultivate at Albukhary International University.

I hope EduCycle will continue to inspire students, educators, parents, and communities to recognise that even simple ideas, when driven by purpose and compassion, can create significant positive change. I congratulate the entire EduCycle team on their dedication and achievements, and I look forward to witnessing the continued growth and impact of this meaningful initiative.

Madam Aliaa Diyana Zamri Advisor, EduCycle School of Business and Social Sciences Albukhary International University
